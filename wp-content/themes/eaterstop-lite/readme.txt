== Description ==
Eaterstop Lite is a free Restaurant WordPress themes specially designed and developed to suit the requirements of restaurant and cafe websites. Eaterstop Lite is a sophisticated and innovative free WordPress theme that can be used for creating different websites for restaurant, cafeteria, pub, bar, wine shop and other food related business.
